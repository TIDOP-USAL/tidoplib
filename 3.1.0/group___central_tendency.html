The mean is the sum of all the data divided by the number of data points. It is represented as:

\[ \overline{x} = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^n x_i}{n} \]

The most commonly used measure of dispersion for the mean is the standard deviation, but it can also be another measure such as the range, coefficient of variation, etc. It is very sensitive to outliers.
