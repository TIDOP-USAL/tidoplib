Detailed Description

DDA Algorithm (Digital Differential Analyzer)

The DDA (Digital Differential Analyzer) algorithm is a rasterization conversion algorithm based on the calculation of either Dy or Dx using one of the equations: \( Dy = m * Dx \)
\( Dx = Dy / m \)
Sampling of the line is performed at unit intervals in one coordinate, and the closest integer values to the line's trajectory are determined for the other coordinate.
